Verizon Technology

Verizon is one of the largest communication technology companies in the world. We operate America’s largest 4G LTE wireless network and the nation’s premiere all-fiber broadband network.

We also operate one of the world's most connected public internet backbone networks, enabling global solutions for secure communication. Our Global IP network connects over 2700 cities in more than 150 countries. More than 5 percent of the internet's traffic flows over our network.
